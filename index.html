What is Tatkal Software ?

Tatkal software is a tool that automates the process of booking Tatkal tickets on Indian Railways. It saves time and effort by automating the tasks of filling in passenger details, selecting the train, and making payment. Tatkal software can be used by travel agents or individuals who frequently book Tatkal tickets and need to book them quickly.
